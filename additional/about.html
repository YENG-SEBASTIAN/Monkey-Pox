The Project

Our project aims to provide an advanced tool for the early detection of monkey pox. We utilize state-of-the-art machine learning models, specifically leveraging the Mobilenet_v2 architecture, to analyze skin lesions and determine their nature.

Technology

We employ deep learning methodologies to train our models. Deep learning is a subset of machine learning, designed to mimic the human brain's processing of data and decision-making capabilities. Specifically, we utilize the Mobilenet_v2 architecture for our models, designed to optimize accuracy while minimizing computational costs.

How to Use The Tool

Using our tool is simple: upload an image of the skin lesion you're concerned about, and our model will analyze it and provide a prediction about whether it's likely to be malignant or benign. Please remember that this tool is not a substitute for professional medical advice. If you're worried about any skin changes, always consult a healthcare professional.
